## Methodological notes

- **Item 5 (registration):** the protocol was not registered externally (e.g. OSF), but is fully documented and frozen in SEARCH_LOG.md before screening - in keeping with the spirit of layer L1 applied to the review itself.
- **Items 12/16 (critical appraisal):** quality assessment of individual works is not required in a scoping review and was deliberately omitted; the limitation of depth (28 of 56 full-text) is explicitly named in §8.6.
- **Item 22 (funding):** the funding declaration and AI involvement statement (in accordance with layer L6) are to be completed in the submission package (phase 11).
